Picadilly Square, or Picadilly Circus, is London’s answer to Times Square – a buzzing street junction in the heart of the city’s shopping and entertainment areas, famous for its neon lights and advertising billboards. This is the perfect place to enjoy one of London’s most iconic photo-ops – the fountain monument dedicated to Lord Shaftesbury (“The Angel of Christian Charity”), with the statue of a Greek god above it. In close proximity to Picadilly Square, you will find the six story indoor amusement arcade, Trocadero, as well as the Criterion Theater, opened in 1874. The beauty of Picadilly is that it provides entertainment for all; from young kids to people watchers, and for those looking for a gourmet meal or simply a drink in an authentic London pub.
